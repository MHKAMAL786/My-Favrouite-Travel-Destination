My Favrouite Travel Destination : Naran, Kaghan

There are many reasons to visit Naran Kaghan! The valley is known for its breathtaking landscapes, lush green meadows, snow-capped mountains, and crystal-clear lakes like Saif-ul-Muluk. It’s a perfect destination for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those who want to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. Plus, the cool weather and friendly locals make it even more inviting. Would you like recommendations on the best places to visit there

Top Attractions

  1. Lake Saif-ul-Muluk – A stunning alpine lake with mesmerizing views. You can enjoy boating and storytelling about its folklore.
  2. Lulusar Lake – A serene lake surrounded by snow-capped peaks, perfect for photography.
  3. Babusar Top – A high mountain pass with breathtaking panoramic views and a cool breeze.
  4. Shogran – A peaceful hill station with lush green meadows and a refreshing climate.

Top Activities To Enjoy

  • Boating – Enjoy a peaceful boat ride on Lake Saif-ul-Muluk.
  • Hiking & Trekking – Explore trails like Dudipatsar Lake and Siri Paye.
  • Camping – Spend a night under the stars in scenic spots like Lalazar.
  • Photography – Capture the breathtaking beauty of the valley.
